  1. Go to your Google Form
  2. Click the preview button (eye icon)

Common Issues:

  • Form not accepting responses (check form settings)
  • Wrong entry IDs (they change when you modify the form)
  • Field types don't match (e.g., trying to send text to a date field)
  • Required fields missing
